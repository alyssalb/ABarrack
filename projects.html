Digital Work

Critical Making

I make weird little things to help people understand what I study — or to help me think through a problem. I approach digital work like writing: it's an alternative way into a problem. My writing practice and my digital making practice speak to each other the way dance and music do. Both are in my body. They're different expressions using distinct skills, working together to make something.

TraceryDigital ArtCare Work

Passing Notes: An Experiment in Digital Care

A generative web artwork using Tracery grammar and Dvorak-to-QWERTY encoding to produce procedural love letters — a public act of queer feminist care, inspired by Design Justice and Your Computer Is On Fire.
